The 6 Triggers

When institutions panic, you profit.

Each trigger represents a moment when institutional risk rules force predictable, mechanical behaviour. These are observable in public data — every single trading day.

T1
9/10 Reliability
VaR Breach — VIX Spike
When India VIX spikes 15%+ in a single session, institutional risk systems auto-trigger position reduction. They must sell regardless of their view. Validated: COVID 2020, Russia-Ukraine 2022.
→ Wait 30–45 min · Buy ATM calls or sell OTM puts
T2
8/10 Reliability
FII Drawdown Limit
FII net short futures above ₹15,000 Cr for 3+ days = drawdown mode. They mechanically reduce risk per internal rules. Validated: October 2024 record ₹1.14 lakh crore selling.
→ Do not buy calls · Wait for FII short reduction signal
T3
7/10 Reliability
Max Pain Expiry Pin
Market makers delta-hedge books on expiry day creating gravitational pull toward Max Pain strike after 1:30 PM. Fires every expiry — most predictable calendar-based trigger.
→ Sell ATM straddle after 1:30 PM · Theta crushes both sides
T4
9/10 Reliability
Margin Call Cascade
All sectors falling together with no news = leveraged positions being margin-called. Not fundamental. Validated: June 2024 election crash — Nifty -5.93%, fully recovered in 27 days.
→ Buy calls in tranches · Recovery historically 3–27 days
T5
10/10 Reliability
Pre-Event IV Crush
Institutions buy options as insurance before RBI, Budget, Elections. They mechanically sell post-event. IV collapses 30–50% within 30 minutes regardless of outcome. Fires 8–10x per year.
→ Sell straddle 1–2 days pre-event · IV crush is mechanical
T6
7/10 Reliability
Macro FII Rule Trigger
USDINR crossing key levels or US 10Y yield spikes trigger automatic EM reallocation in global funds. India sold non-discretionarily — nothing to do with India fundamentals.
→ Watch USDINR + US 10Y · Wait for stabilisation before buying

Questions

Common questions.

Is this for beginners or experienced traders?
+
Both. Beginners benefit from the clear framework — 5 numbers, one signal, plain English instruction. Experienced traders benefit from the institutional intelligence layer which reframes everything they already know. The paper trading journal is designed to build evidence regardless of experience level.
Do I need coding knowledge to use the Algo Framework?
+
No. The dashboard and journal are fully browser-based — no code required. The Algo Framework includes copy-paste ready Python code for Dhan and Zerodha APIs, and Pine Script you paste directly into TradingView. You need to add your API keys, but the logic is complete.
How much capital do I need to start?
+
The framework works with any capital size because it's percentage-based (max 2% per trade). Paper trading requires zero capital. For live trading, even ₹50,000 is sufficient to apply the trigger-based position sizing framework. The rule is never more than 2% of capital per trade — so your capital determines your absolute rupee risk, not whether you can participate.
Are the triggers validated with real historical data?
+
Yes. All 6 triggers are backtested against real Indian market events — COVID 2020, Russia-Ukraine 2022, June 2024 election crash, October 2024 record FII selling, and every RBI policy event. The historical event dashboard in the Journal tool shows trigger vs outcome for each validated event.
Will this work when everyone starts using it?
+
Unlike conventional indicators, these triggers are based on institutional rule structures that exist independent of how many retail traders know about them. FII drawdown limits, VaR breach rules, and margin cascade mechanics are structural — they'll fire whenever institutional stress occurs regardless of what retail does. The edge comes from reading the forced behaviour, not from information asymmetry alone.
Is this SEBI registered investment advice?
+
No. IntelligentTraders is an educational and research framework — not SEBI registered investment advice. The tools are for educational purposes. All trading decisions are your own responsibility. We strongly recommend paper trading for a minimum of 90 days before deploying real capital using any framework including this one.
